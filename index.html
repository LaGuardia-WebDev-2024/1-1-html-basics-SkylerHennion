Poem about stuff

copied from the internet :O

I pondered long and deep
About words to set in motion
But as my pen took its leap
I found a curious notion
This poem, I did not write
Its words are not my own
Yet still, they dance in light
In a melody unknown.

It speaks of a different way
Of seeing the world around
A perspective caught in play
In a realm profound

Perhaps it comes from above
A gift from the divine
A message of love
In every line

I may not have penned it
But I feel its essence true
In every word, every bit
I find a piece of you

So I'll embrace this unwritten verse
With an open heart and mind
 For in its beauty I immerse
A treasure rare to find

Ode to the unwritten poem
I thank you for your presence
In your mystery, I find home
In your words, I find essence.
